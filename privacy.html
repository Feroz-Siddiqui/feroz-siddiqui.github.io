Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Aisha Rasheed's Educational Services. We are dedicated to protecting your privacy and securing the personal information you provide to us. This Privacy Policy explains the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, the measures we take to keep your information safe, and the rights you have regarding your personal information. Our practices are in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant privacy laws.

Information We Collect

To provide and improve our educational services, we collect information in the following categories:

  • Provided by you: This includes any information you give us directly, such as when you sign up for our services, contact us for support, or interact with our website and Android app. For example, we may collect your name, email address (aishaoceanic@gmail.com), and phone number (+91-9070838480).
  • Collected automatically: When you use our services, we automatically gather information such as app usage statistics, IP addresses, and browser types. This helps us understand how our services are used and how we can improve them.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is vital for delivering and enhancing our services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Provide you with personalized and efficient educational services.
  • Develop new features and offerings based on user feedback and usage patterns.
  • Offer content tailored to your preferences and interests.
  • Ensure our service's security and compliance with legal and regulatory obligations.

Sharing Your Information

Your privacy is crucial to us, which means we share your personal information only under certain conditions:

  • With your consent: We will share personal information when we have your explicit consent to do so.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law or in response to legal requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have rights that allow you to control your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request the correction of inaccurate information about you.
  • The right to request the deletion of your information when it's no longer necessary for the purposes collected.
  • The right to object to our processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • The right to request that we limit the processing of your personal data.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access control, secure software development practices, and regular security assessments.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will post any policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice. We will also keep prior versions of this Privacy Policy in an archive for your review.

Data Protection Principles

We adhere to the principles of data protection outlined by GDPR, ensuring that all personal data is:

  • Processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner.
  • Collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
  • Adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.
  • Accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date.
  • Stored in a form which allows identification of data subjects for no longer than necessary.
  • Processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security.

Personal Data/Files

In alignment with GDPR, we ensure the secure storage and processing of personal data. This includes the careful management of personal files, sensitive information, and respecting data subjects' rights to privacy.
